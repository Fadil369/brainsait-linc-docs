### Logo Guidelines

**DO**:
- ‚úÖ Use official logo files only
- ‚úÖ Maintain minimum clear space
- ‚úÖ Use approved color variations
- ‚úÖ Maintain aspect ratio
- ‚úÖ Ensure adequate contrast

**DON'T**:
- ‚ùå Stretch or distort the logo
- ‚ùå Change logo colors

## üìê Design Principles

### Visual Hierarchy

1. **Primary Information**: Largest, boldest, primary color
2. **Secondary Information**: Medium size, regular weight
3. **Tertiary Information**: Smaller size, lighter weight

### Spacing

Use consistent spacing based on 8px grid:
- Small spacing: 8px
- Medium spacing: 16px
- Large spacing: 24px
- Extra large spacing: 32px

### Accessibility

!!! warning "WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance"
    - **Color Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text
    - **Large Text**: Minimum 3:1 for 18pt+ text
    - **Interactive Elements**: Minimum 44x44px touch targets
    - **Alt Text**: Provide for all images
    - **Keyboard Navigation**: Ensure full keyboard accessibility

## ‚úçÔ∏è Writing Style

### Tone of Voice

**Professional** yet **Approachable**
- Clear and concise language
- Active voice preferred
- Avoid unnecessary jargon
- Explain technical terms

### Bilingual Content

#### English Content
- Use international English spelling
- Medical terminology following SNOMED CT
- Clear and direct sentences

#### Arabic Content
- Modern Standard Arabic (MSA)
- Right-to-left (RTL) layout
- Proper medical terminology
- Cultural sensitivity

### Content Guidelines

**DO**:
- ‚úÖ Use inclusive language
- ‚úÖ Write in active voice
- ‚úÖ Break up long paragraphs
- ‚úÖ Use bullet points for lists
- ‚úÖ Provide examples

**DON'T**:
- ‚ùå Use all caps (except abbreviations)
- ‚ùå Overuse exclamation marks
- ‚ùå Use technical jargon without explanation
- ‚ùå Make unsupported claims
- ‚ùå Use ambiguous language
